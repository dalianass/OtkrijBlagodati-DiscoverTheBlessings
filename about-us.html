Few words about us

About us:

Discover Blessings makes a team of 10 people. Most of them are female students. All our texts first go through the sharia review of hfz. prof. Halida Dacić Dazdarević, and after that proofreading. We are witnessing that we are too accustomed to the blessings that pass by us almost unnoticed. Therefore, our guiding idea is for us all to be more focused on them together, and to recognize them more in our daily lives. Our WhatsApp group is designed so that texts are sent every day at 9:00 am, so that readers can start their morning by thinking about grace. After every ninth text, a table is sent with examples of gratitude for the blessings that have been written about in the previous nine days. The group is not limited to the writing of our regular authors, but each member is given the opportunity to send their text about grace to our email, and any text that meets the criteria of sharia review will be shared on the group and our social networks. The ultimate goal of our action is greater gratitude to Allahu ta'ala for blessings, so we ask him to open our minds, and make us grateful.
